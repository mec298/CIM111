Who we are

Despite advances in many other service industries such as Airbnb, the vacation rental service industry or Expedia and the travel booking industry, pet sitting is a niche in the service market that has yet to see much advancement in innovation over the last decade. With PetGo, pet sitting finally works with a modern model more akin to Travelocity or Uber. With PetGo, Pet owners are able to find and book the optimal and highest quality care services for their furry, feathered, finned, or scaled friends online quickly and efficiently. PetGo not only caters to pet owners, but the freelance compensation model allows for both commercial operations and individual pet sitters to offer their services on an online platform.
